Allama Muhammad Iqbal

One of the greatest poet!

Here's a time line of Allama Muhammad Iqbal life:

  • 1877 - Born in Sialkot, Punjab.
  • 1893 - Matriculated at Scotch Mission College."
  • 1895 - received an Intermediate level with the Faculty of Arts diploma.
  • 1897 - Bachelor of Arts in philosophy, English literature and Arabic.
  • 1899 - Received his Master of Arts degree from the same college and won first place in philosophy in the University of the Punjab.
  • 1905 - Travelled to England to pursue higher education.During his study in Europe, Iqbal began to write poetry in Persian.
  • 1933 - After returning from a trip to Spain and Afghanistan, Iqbal suffered from a mysterious throat illness.
